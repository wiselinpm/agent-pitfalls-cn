## 摘要

<p>You can now generate time-bound signed URLs for <a href="https://vercel.com/docs/vercel-blob">Vercel Blob</a>. A signed URL is a scoped URL with an expiry that allows you to upload, download, inspect, or delete a specific object without giving access to your entire Blob store.
