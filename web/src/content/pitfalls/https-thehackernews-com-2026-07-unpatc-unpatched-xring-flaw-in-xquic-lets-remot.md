## 摘要

A single wrong variable on one line in XQUIC, Alibaba's QUIC and HTTP/3 library, lets any remote client crash the server with a short burst of completely legal traffic. There is no patch. FoxIO researcher Sébastien Féry&nbsp;disclosed the flaw on July 8&nbsp;and nicknamed it XRIN
