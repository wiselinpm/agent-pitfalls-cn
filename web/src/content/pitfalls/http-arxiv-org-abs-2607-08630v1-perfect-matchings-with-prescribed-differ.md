## 摘要

The Balister--Győri--Schelp (BGS) conjecture asks whether every zero-sum list of $2^{s-1}$ nonzero vectors in $\mathbb{F}_2^s$ is the prescribed-difference profile of a perfect matching. The conjecture remains open in general, whereas the classical Hall hyperplane case is solved
