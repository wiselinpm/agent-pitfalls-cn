<p>We’ve updated Log Drains pricing on all Pro and Enterprise plans, reducing the charge increments.</p><p>Data transferred for Log Drains will be billed at $0.50 per 1GB, instead of the previous $10 per 20GB, providing more precise usage tracking and better cost efficiency.</p><
