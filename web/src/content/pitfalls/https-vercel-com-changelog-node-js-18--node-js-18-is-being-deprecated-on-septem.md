## 摘要

<p>Following the Node.js 18 end of life on April 30, 2025, we are deprecating Node.js 18 for Builds and Functions on September 1, 2025.</p><p><b>Will my existing deployments be affected?</b></p><p>No, existing deployments with Serverless Functions <b>will not be affected.</b></p>
