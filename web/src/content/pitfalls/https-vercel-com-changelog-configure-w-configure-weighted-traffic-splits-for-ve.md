<p>You can now configure weighted traffic splits for <a href="https://vercel.com/docs/vercel-flags">Vercel Flags</a> with the new <code>vercel flags split</code> command in the Vercel CLI. This allows you to send a percentage of traffic to one variant and the rest to another.</p>
