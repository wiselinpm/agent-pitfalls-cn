## 摘要

Amazon Elastic Kubernetes Service (Amazon EKS) Auto Mode now offers significantly reduced management fees for GPU and accelerated instance types. Beginning July 1, 2026, G-series Auto Mode management fees are reduced by 35%, and P-series and AWS Trainium fees are reduced by 60%.
